Collection of Assignments for CSE 3000: Contemporary Issues in Computer Science and Engineering


Based on my readings in chapter 5, my ethical statement is as follows:
1. Curiousity is paramount as it's the most meaningful way to grow. A lack of curiosity leads to a stagnant thinking process.
2. Open communication is how all communication should be, as being open is the best way to understand other people. Closed communication is only necessary with messages over technology, as that denotes security and not a lack of understanding.
3. Holding myself accountable is important. Use the previous two points to ask people how they feel about my actions and think to ask about it.
4. Reliability is a testament to your connections with other people, and should be held up not only through big acts a few times, but small acts over a long time.
5. The most important of all is respect in every sense. Respect for other people and what they're going through, respect for the world around you. Holding respect for everything and everyone you interact with, even when it seems hard, is what's needed for everyone.
